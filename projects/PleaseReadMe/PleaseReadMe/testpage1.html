A Blog?

What's this blog?

Some crazy girl went on an adventure, and when her visa ran out, she went on an even bigger adventure. That blog is a partial chronicle of her travels. It's widely boring.

Should I read it?

Honestly, best not. It's not exactly been upkept or updated in over 8 months. Everest is why. It's hard to write about mountains that resonate with the energy of the depths of the earth.

So why does it exist?

Someone asked her to write it. But she sucks at it, which is why it's rarely updated. It's too tedious to find the right words to describe adventure.

Is this goodbye then?

No, never. She doesn't say goodbye, only "See you later" or- only sometimes- "See you soon"
